What is a routine entry?

Whether booked monthly, quarterly or at year-end, regular items are usually periodic in nature. Routine entries result from the normal on-going business of the Company, such as regular invoices or accruals. They are executional in nature and do not require a large degree of judgment as to timing of recognition and amount

What is a Non Routine entry?

Generally require additional consideration, scrutiny and authorization, from both the Business and Accounting organization. Unusual transactions are not expected to reoccur with any regular frequency. They are not regularly handled as part of the closing process. Non-routine transactions occur as a result of non standard business activity and are often unique.

What are the general approval limits for JE?

See GA2 for complete guidelines. General approval limits are:
  • Items < USD 100M - No fomal approval required.
  • Items >= USD 100M and < USD 2MM - Accounting Transaction Owner (Manager of the individual posting the entry)
  • Items >= USD 2MM and > USD 5MM - One up manager of the indivudual posting the entry (at least Band 2), Business Transaction Owner and Associate Director/Director Region Comptroller Group
  • Items > USD 5MM - Two Up manager of the Accounting Transaction Owner (at least Band 3), Business Transaction Owner and VP Corporate Accounting (via Region Comptroller Group)

Is a SAP screenshot a valid support documentation/ What is considered valid support documentation?

Support documentation should be sufficient to allow an independent person without intimate knowledge of the operations to arrive at the same accounting conclusion as the originator and accounting reviewers. Should be relevant and sufficient to justify the validity of the entry and the amount. Key items are: reason for the entry, basis for calculation, proper accounting period and appropriate authorizations

How do I know who is the business transaction owner?

Is the person with product responsibility (ie. Category Finance Manager). In BUs, the Regional or Global PS F&A Associate Director for the business unit may be designated as the Business Transaction Owner for TDC transactions. The person with Profit responsibility may delegate their approval to their Regional Central Forecast Team Band 3 manager, up to $500M. If multiple GBUs, Tax or a Corporate profit center are involved, someone from Corporate Accounting, Tax or Legal Entity Steward becomes the owner.
