Approval and review process

The business/entry requestor has primary responsibility to ensure that the entry is appropriate, analysis is supported with documentation, is in compliance with SAFE and local/US GAAP.
Non-routine postings of >= $100M. Entry requestor is responsible for obtaining approval from the Business Transaction. The accounting review should focus on the accounts, timing, and reasonability.
Routine postings and non-routine postings less than $100M. Reconciliations should be in place to ensure the integrity of the financial statements. If the Accounting Processor is concerned about any entry, then he/she should elevate the entry for additional reviews prior to posting.
