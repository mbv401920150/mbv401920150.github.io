Appropriate Tax authorization should be obtained for non-routine tax adjustments

Booking of quarterly or annual tax provisions does not fall under these authorization limits.

Examples of tax entries that require approval are:

  1. Tax reserves (both income and indirect tax reserves)
  2. Changes to a deferred tax balance that is not the result of a quarterly or year-end provision.
  3. Tax entries related to acquisitions or divestitures.

Any other entries that are outside the provision process.
