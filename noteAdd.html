Business transaction owner

Is the person with product responsibility (ie. the Category Finance Manager). In BUs where central PS F&A organizations exists with full TDC budgeting responsibility, the Regional or Global PS F&A Associate Director for the business unit may be designated as the Business Transaction Owner for TDC transactions. The person with Profit responsibility (ie. the Category Finance Manager) may delegate their approval to their Regional Central Forecast Team (CFT) Band 3 manager, up to $500M.

The Business Transaction Owner should be at the highest level available but no lower than B3.
In the case where multiple GBUs, Tax or a Corporate profit center is involved, someone from Corporate Accounting, Tax or Legal Entity Steward becomes the owner (see additional considerations section). Please note that each business may decide to require more stringent authorization requirements that the ones noted above.
