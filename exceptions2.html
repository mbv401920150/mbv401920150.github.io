Different Month Correcting Entries with No Impact on External Reporting: No Additional Authorization Required

Requires appropriate approvals and accounting reviews unless they meet all three of the following criteria:

  1. No net P&L impact to the Company
  2. No impact to externally reportable segment results (i.e., Beauty Care, Fabric and Home Care, etc.)
  3. No impact to externally reportable line items on the financial statements (Balance Sheet, Income Statement) or footnote disclosures.

This does not apply if the correction relates to the P&L and is not within the same fiscal year (requires review with Corp. Accounting organization)
