Local GAAP journals

Local GAAP Journals will follow the intent of US GAAP journals with regard to definition of routine and non-routine journals. The approval authorizers will be the main difference.
Routine entries should be documented, but no approval will be needed for these entries. New routine entries will have to be approved the first time they occur only.
Due to the possible low materiality of many legal entities, the Steward and/or Country Tax Manager can request informally to receive copies of journals of a lower value.
