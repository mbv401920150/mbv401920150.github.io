Principles for documentation and Maintenance of documentation

Should be sufficient enough to allow a person without intimate knowledge of the operations to arrive at the same accounting conclusion as the originator and accounting reviewer.
Supporting Documentation Maintained by the Service Center - The person who books the entry is responsible for ensuring the appropriate documentation is on file for each journal entry processed.
Supporting Documentation Maintained outside the Service Center- The entry requestor should provide the accounting transaction processor with sufficient information to perform the accounting review. This may be a one-page summary or email that will be filed by the person posting the entry. Additional supporting documentation will be filed by entry requestor outside of the Service Center.
Documentation for Tax (Income) Contingencies: a journal entry with appropriate authorization is sufficient documentation for Income Tax Contingencies and will be retained by Global Tax.
For year-end MSA/SRA accruals may be held in the local markets (without an exception), because of the volume of supporting documentation.
